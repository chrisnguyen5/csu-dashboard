Overview

The purpose of this page is to allow users to track the progress of CSU campuses as they work to meet their 2025 graduation initiative goals. The views on this page show the graduation rates of cohorts over time in relation to a linear path of interim targets that extends to the 2025 graduation initiative goal rates.
